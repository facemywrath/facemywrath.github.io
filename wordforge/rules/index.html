WordForge Rules

How to Play

In WordForge, your goal is to create valid words from a random pool of letters. Each letter has a cooldown, and you must strategically build your words while managing the cooldowns. The more words you create, the more points you score!

Rules

  • Create valid words by clicking on the letters in the pool.
  • Once you submit a word, the letters used will enter cooldown and cannot be reused until the cooldown ends.
  • Your score is based on the length of the words you submit. Longer words score higher.
  • Every 10 words submitted, a letter is removed from the pool until the minimum number of letters is reached.
  • If a word is invalid, it will not be accepted and your word progress will reset.

Objectives

Your primary objectives are:

  • Submit as many valid words as possible.
  • Keep your score increasing to beat your high score.
  • Manage your available letters efficiently to maximize your word-building potential.

Scoring

  • Each valid word you submit adds to your score. The score is calculated based on word length.
  • Track your progress with the progress bar, which resets after every 10 words submitted.

Tips

  • Use vowels and common consonants wisely to create longer words.
  • Plan your next words while managing letter cooldowns effectively.
  • Try to submit longer words for higher scores, but be mindful of cooldowns.
